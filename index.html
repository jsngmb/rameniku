The Story

A ramen house concept designed around energy, warmth, and repeat-worthy flavor.

Rameniku blends the playful punch of a modern food brand with the comfort of a late-night ramen spot. The direction leans into warm broth tones, dark interiors, and a menu built for both casual cravings and dine-in moments that feel memorable.

From the visual identity to the landing page experience, the goal is to make the brand feel bold, craveable, and easy to remember the moment it lands on screen.
